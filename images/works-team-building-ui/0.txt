--- English ---
Client:NEC Corporation
Title:Team Building UI/UX Design
Description:Created designs for business development verification of a meeting room and recreation reservation service aimed at employee team building. At SEESAW, we handle everything from concept tagline development to UI design and design system creation. We use vibrant color schemes and three-dimensional pop illustrations to create visuals that foster excitement through recreation.
