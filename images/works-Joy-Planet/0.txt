--- English ---
Client:
Title:Joy Planet Logo Web Design
Description:Designed the official website for "Joy Planet," a playground where parents and children can have fun together. With the theme of "play = learning," we used colorful and pop visuals with playful geometric motifs like circles and triangles to balance children's excitement with a sense of security.
